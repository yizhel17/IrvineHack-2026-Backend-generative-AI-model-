Please be advised that all delinquent taxes, assessments, penalties, and costs are accumulating daily interest at the rate of one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month. Failure to redeem this property by paying the full amount due will result in the loss of your right to redeem the property and may lead to its sale at a public tax auction, pursuant to California Revenue and Taxation Code Sections 3691 and 3707.

The five-year redemption period from the date of default for the 2018 defaulted taxes concluded on June 30, 2023.
